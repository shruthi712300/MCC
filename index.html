Our Mission & Values

MCC exists to empower the Malaysian Ceylonese community through education, cultural preservation, social welfare and civic participation while furthering national unity and shared prosperity.

  • Integrity

    Transparent and accountable leadership in every endeavour.

  • Unity

    Fostering common ground and shared destiny across communities.

  • Empowerment

    Encouraging active participation, leadership, and growth within the Malaysian Ceylonese community to create future-ready citizens.

  • Service (Seva)

    Putting people first and generating tangible impact.

  • Excellence

    Pursuing high standards in education, culture and governance.

  • Heritage

    Preserving and celebrating the rich cultural, linguistic, and historical legacy of the Malaysian Ceylonese community for future generations.
