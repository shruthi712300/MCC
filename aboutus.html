Founding & History

MCC was established as a political party with the aim of giving support to the then Alliance party. MCC has continuously supported the Barisan National. MCC was the brainchild of the late Mr. M.W Navaratnam and was formed to promote and preserve the Political, Educational, Economical, Social and Cultural aspects of the Malaysians of Ceylonese origin, or Sri Lankan, descent. To date,, six (6) presidents have held office since 1958. Mr M.W Navaratnam (1958–1969), Senator Tan Sri Dr. C. Sinnadurai (1970–1983), Tan Sri Dato' Seri V. Jeyaratnam (1983–1987), Dato Dr N. Arumugasamy (Neurosurgeon) (1988–1995), Datuk Dr. D.M. Thuraiapah (1996–2003 and Dato' Dr NKS Tharmaseelan (2004–Present)
